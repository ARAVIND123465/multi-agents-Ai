# Product FAQ

## Data and security
- Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256).
- EU data residency available on Enterprise.
- You can export workspace data as JSON from Settings → Data.

## Integrations
- Webhooks for ticket and billing events on Growth+.
- Official SDKs: JavaScript/TypeScript and Python.

## Uptime and maintenance
- Status page lists active incidents and scheduled maintenance windows.
- Critical fixes may be deployed outside the regular window with advance notice when possible.

## Refunds
- Monthly plans: prorated refunds for billing errors reported within 14 days.
- Annual plans: reviewed case-by-case; charge disputes should also be raised with your card issuer.
